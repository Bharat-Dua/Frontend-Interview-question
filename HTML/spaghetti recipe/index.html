Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add Spaghetti to boiled water.
    1. Add salt to taste.
    2. Cook for 10 minutes,stirring occasionally.
  3. Add marinara sauce in a pan, bringing to a simmer.
  4. Mix cooked Spaghetti with marinara sauce.
  5. Enjoy
